Gawad Kalinga

Kalinga for Change is a community-driven initiative dedicated to fostering sustainable development and empowering marginalized communities in the Philippines.

KALINGA FOR CHANGE

  • Each community kitchen is established through the collaborative efforts of parents, volunteers, local government units, schools, private individuals, organizations and corporate partners. Community kitchens are fueled and sustained by bayanihan (solidarity and teamwork) as a living proof that collective caring and sharing is the key to ending hunger among children.
